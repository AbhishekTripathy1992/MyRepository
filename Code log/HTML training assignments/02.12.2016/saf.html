The Sundarbans (Bengali: সুন্দরবন, Shundorbôn) is a natural region in Bengal.It is the largest single block of tidal halophytic mangrove forest in the world. The Sundarbans covers approximately 4000 sq kms area is in India. The Sundarbans is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.The Sundarbans National Park is a National Park,Tiger Reserve, and a Biosphere Reserve located in the Sundarbans delta in the Indian state of West Bengal.This region is densely covered by mangrove forests, and is one of thelargest reserves for the Bengal tiger, Saltwater Crocodile, Spotted DeerWild Boar, Rhesus Monkey, over 100 species of Birds,Snakes and various species of Marine life.We are the maniacs who revere nature in its truest form-Raw. We find music in the orchestra of cicadas and do not mind getting lost in the trails of unknown, among the greens. From the stripes to the spots, from the hooves to the tusks, from the ones that glide over the clouds to the ones that slide under the ground we revel them all.
